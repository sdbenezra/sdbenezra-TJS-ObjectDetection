Object Detection Demo

Model may take a few seconds to download when you first load this page.
Upload a picture using the button that appears below.
Objects will be identified, boxed, and labeled after analyzing.

It may take a minute or two for the analysis to occur, and it's still experimental. If you don't see results in a minute, try uploading a different picture, with one or two items and an uncluttered background.
It also tends to run faster on a computer rather than mobile.
Any pictures you upload will stay on your machine, they are not sent over the internet.
